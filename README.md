## Project Overview

This project is completed as part of the CodeAlpha Civil Engineering Internship. The project focuses on understanding civil engineering concepts, structural design principles, and the process of creating safe and efficient building structures.

The main objective of this task is to apply engineering design concepts, analyze structural elements, and understand the importance of planning, design, and safety in civil engineering projects.

## About Civil Engineering and Structural Design

Civil engineering is a field of engineering that deals with the planning, design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as buildings, bridges, roads, and other structures.

Structural design focuses on designing components that can safely withstand applied loads while maintaining strength, stability, and durability.

## Structural Components Studied

### 1. Beams

Beams are horizontal structural elements that transfer loads to supporting columns or walls.

### 2. Columns

Columns are vertical structural elements responsible for carrying compressive loads.

### 3. Slabs

Slabs provide floor and roof surfaces while transferring loads to beams and columns.

### 4. Foundations

Foundations transfer structural loads safely to the ground and provide stability.
